Built From The Mal3ab

DunkFloor is a Lebanese street basketball platform created to represent the real game. Not the polished version — the real one. The one played on outdoor Mal3ab, in neighborhoods, under pressure, with pride.

The Idea

Across Lebanon, there are talented La3ibe and strong Mal3ab, but most of them stay unseen. DunkFloor was created to give them visibility.

The Culture

Street basketball is not just a game. It’s competition, respect, and identity built through real games — not highlights.

The Goal

The goal of DunkFloor is simple: connect La3ibe, showcase Mal3ab, and build a real platform for Lebanese street basketball.
